FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions About Sunrise & Sunset Data

Everything you need to know about SunriseTime's accuracy, coverage, and data sources.

How accurate is SunriseTime data?

SunriseTime uses the NOAA Solar Calculator algorithm, the same standard used by the U.S. National Oceanic and Atmospheric Administration. Our sunrise and sunset predictions are accurate to within 1 minute for any location worldwide, accounting for latitude, longitude, altitude, and atmospheric refraction.

How many cities does SunriseTime cover?

SunriseTime covers over 5,000 cities globally, sourced from the GeoNames geographical database. This includes all major metropolitan areas, popular photography destinations, and cities with over 50,000 population. We're continuously expanding coverage based on user demand.

What is golden hour and why does it matter?

Golden hour is the period shortly after sunrise and before sunset when the sun is low on the horizon, creating warm, soft, directional light. It's considered the best time for photography, filmmaking, and outdoor activities. SunriseTime calculates the exact golden hour window for your location, so you never miss that perfect light.

How are prayer times calculated?

Prayer times are calculated using the PrayTimes algorithm, which supports multiple juristic methods including University of Islamic Sciences (Karachi), Islamic Society of North America (ISNA), Muslim World League, and Umm al-Qura. You can select your preferred calculation method for the most accurate results.

Can I access SunriseTime data via API?

Yes! SunriseTime provides a free REST API that returns JSON data for sunrise, sunset, golden hour, moon phase, and prayer times for any city and date. The API is open to developers who want to integrate sun data into their own applications. Check our API documentation for integration guides and rate limits.

Is SunriseTime free to use?

Absolutely! SunriseTime is 100% free to use — no account required, no hidden fees. All features including sunrise/sunset times, golden hour tracking, moon phase data, prayer time calculations, and the API are completely free for everyone. We sustain the platform through unobtrusive advertising.
